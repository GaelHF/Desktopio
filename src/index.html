Territorial.io

Territorial.io is a fast-paced, competitive multiplayer strategy game focused on territorial conquest, where the ultimate goal is to control as much of the game map as possible.

Agar.io

Agar.io is a massive multiplayer online action game created by Brazilian developer Matheus Valadares. Players control one or more circular cells in a map representing a Petri dish..

Slither.io

Players control an avatar resembling a snake, which consumes multi-colored pellets, both from other players and ones that naturally spawn on the map in the game, to grow in size. The objective of the game is to grow the longest snake in the server.

Skribbl.io

skribbl.io is a free online multiplayer drawing and guessing pictionary game. A normal game consists of a few rounds, where every round a player has to draw their chosen word and others have to guess it to gain points! Draw and have fun.
